Our Services

Project Management

A project management role involves planning, organizing, and overseeing tasks and resources to ensure a project is completed on time and within budget, while meeting its objectives and quality standards.

Supplying machinery

Supplying machinery for a mineral water plant involves providing the necessary equipment and technology required for the production of bottled mineral water. This typically includes various stages and components such as water purification, bottling, labeling, and packaging.

Building Construction

Constructing a mineral water plant involves the design and construction of a facility that can purify, package, and store mineral water for commercial distribution, works included Civil, Mechanical and Electrical works

Licensing

In India, setting up a mineral water plant involves obtaining several licenses and complying with regulatory requirements. The specific licenses and permits you may need can vary depending on the location, scale, and nature of your plan

Operation & Maintenance

Operating and maintaining a mineral water plant involves purifying water, bottling it, and ensuring equipment is functional and clean to meet regulatory standards.

Sales & Marketing:

Sales and marketing for a mineral water plant entail promoting the product, finding distribution channels, and building brand awareness to attract customers and grow revenue.
